Introduction

Bears, which are adorable, robust, and magnificent creatures, are mammals belonging to the Ursidae family and typically exhibit an omnivorous diet. There is a diverse array of bear species, encompassing brown bears, there's...

  • polar bears
  • grizzly bears
  • sloth bears
  • giant panda bears
  • cinnamon bears
  • sun bears

...and numerous others. According to National Geographic, bears inhabit various regions across North America, South America, Asia, and Europe. In the wild, bears generally have an average lifespan of 25 years, which can double under captive conditions ("Bears: Grizzly, Polar, Panda," n.d.). Sadly, numerous factors pose threats and jeopardize the well-being and survival of these animals.
